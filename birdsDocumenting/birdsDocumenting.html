The Great Indian Birding Expedition: Documenting Nation's Avian Treasures

A vast landscapes and diverse habitats, I'm on a mission to document the incredible avian diversity of India. From the majestic Himalayan Griffon soaring over snow-capped peaks to the vibrant Indian Peafowl strutting through lush forests, each bird encounter adds another piece to the puzzle. With 98 species already identified, this project aims to create a comprehensive national bird registry, serving as a valuable resource for researchers, conservationists, and bird enthusiasts alike. Join me on this exciting journey of discovery as we unlock the secrets of India's feathered friends!
