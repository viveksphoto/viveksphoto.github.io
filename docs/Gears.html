Cameras

Canon EOS RP

The Canon EOS RP is a compact and lightweight full-frame mirrorless camera designed for both enthusiasts and professionals. It features a 26.2-megapixel sensor, fast autofocus with Dual Pixel CMOS AF, and 4K video recording. The camera is known for its portability, user-friendly interface, and compatibility with a wide range of RF and EF lenses (with an adapter). It's a great choice for photographers looking for high-quality images in a smaller, more affordable package.

Canon 700D

The Canon EOS 700D (also known as the Rebel T5i) is a versatile DSLR camera featuring an 18-megapixel APS-C CMOS sensor and a DIGIC 5 image processor. It offers full HD video recording, a 9-point autofocus system, and a 3-inch vari-angle touchscreen LCD for intuitive control. The EOS 700D is known for its ease of use, making it a solid choice for beginner and intermediate photographers looking to explore DSLR photography while delivering high-quality images.

Drones

Dji Mini 2

The DJI Mini 2 is a compact and lightweight drone designed for aerial photography and videography. It features a 12MP camera capable of shooting 4K video at 30fps, providing crisp and clear footage. With a maximum flight time of 31 minutes and a transmission range of up to 10 km.
